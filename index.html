Getting Started

Your step-by-step guide to get access to your Simplicity® Tenant Portal for new customers.

Click on numbers to view screens.

  • 1

    The first time you log in to the system, it will run a validation check on your online tenant portal account. This validation is done from your email that you have received previously.

  • 2

    Please click on the link in your email from the Tenant portal will take you to the following ‘Reset Password’ prompt.

  • 3

    Please type in a password remembering to have lower and capital letters numbers. You will have to do this twice once done click the ‘Reset Password’ prompt.

  • 4

    You will now be able to log in using your User Name and your new password. Once you are in the Tenant Portal, you will be able to see.

  • 5

    If you need further assistance, please click on the links below and you will be able to watch how to set up contract details, customer user account, permit request, ad hoc utility request, bills and notices, Service Tickets and E-form submission.
