Simplicity Tenant Portal

Learn how to use the Simplicity® Tenant Portal to view your contract details, download your financial documents. See how to submit renovation permits and requests.

See how to use the portal access and manage your tenant account. Contract details, customer user, permit request, Adhoc utility request/cancellation, bills and notices, e-forms, announcements.

Functions of Simplicity® Tenant Portal:

  • Contract Details

    View your Lease/License/Utility Contract details and update Contact listing

  • Customer User Account

    Manage user accounts and grant the relevant access. Inactivation of accounts and reset of password.

  • Permit Request

    Submit renovation permit request covering permit request, extension, permit work request, worker amendment.

  • Adhoc Utility Request

    Submit Request for Ad Hoc Aircon Extension and Cancellation

  • Bills and Notices

    Check your monthly bills and never miss an important notice.

  • Ticket

    Whether it’s an incident, a compliment for a vendor or staff member, or a facility/building fault, you can easily submit service tickets for assistance.

  • E-Form

    Say goodbye to paperwork. Complete and submit forms online, whether you need maintenance or want to provide feedback.
